Black Bean and Tofu Tacos

Description

Tacos with a blend of tofu, black beans, lettuce, tomato, and avocado.

Makes 8 servings

Prep time: 20-30 minutes

Cook time: 20-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 16oz package of firm tofu, drained and cut into small cubes
  • 1 16oz can black beans
  • 16oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 avocado, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 8 taco shells, heated
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Taco sauce
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare tofu, mushrooms, tomatoes, onion, and lettuce.
  2. Add vegetable oil to large pan or skillet. Preheat over low heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms to pan and cook on low heat, 5-10 minutes.
  4. Add tofu, onion, black beans, and taco seasoning to pan. Cook 8-12 minutes.
  5. Prepare avocado.
  6. Spoon mixture into taco shells. Top with lettuce, tomato, avocado, and taco sauce. Serve.
